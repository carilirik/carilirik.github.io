Pembahasan

Chord atau nama purenya adalah akor yaitu sekumpulan nada-nada yang digabungkan secara bersama sehingga menimbulkan bunyi yang harmonis. Dalam alat musik gitar, ada beberapa kunci dasar dalam akord, diantaranya :

Kunci C, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 2 senar (1st) , 4 senar (2rd), dan 5 senar (3th) (fret)Kunci D, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 3 senar (1st), 1 senar (2rd), dan 2 senar (3th) (fret)Kunci E, yaitu kunci yang digunakan saat adanya 3 senar (1st), 5 (2rd), 4 (3th).

Ada juga kunci-kunci lain biasa digunakan yaitu :

Kunci F.Kunci G.Kunci A.Kunci Dm <D minor>.Kunci Em <E minor>.Kunci Am <A minor>.

35. the name of sign is........pliss bantu!!​


That little "a" with a circle curling around it that is found in email addresses is most commonly referred to as the "at" symbol. Surprisingly though, there is no official, universal name for this sign. There are dozens of strange terms to describe the @ symbol.

The tilde is used as a diacritical mark over various letters to indicate a variety of sounds in different languages, but it also appears midline, like a dash (and is sometimes called a swung dash), to denote “approximately (“Last night's attendance: ~100”).

The at sign, @, is normally read aloud as "at"; it is also commonly called the at symbol or commercial at. It is used as an accounting and invoice abbreviation meaning "at a rate of" (e.g. 7 widgets @ £2 per widget = £14), but it is now seen more widely in email addresses and social media platform handles.

What's sign mean?

any object, action, event, pattern, etc., that conveys a meaning. a conventional or arbitrary mark, figure, or symbol used as an abbreviation for the word or words it represents. a motion or gesture used to express or convey an idea, command, decision, etc.: Her nod was a sign that it was time to leave.
