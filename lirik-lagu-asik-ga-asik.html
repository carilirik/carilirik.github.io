28. jawaban nya gimana sih asik asik joss​


Jawaban:

[tex] log_{2}(8) - log_{2}(3) + log_{2}(6 ) \\ log_{2}( \frac{8.6}{3} ) \\ log_{2}(16) \\ log_{2}( {2}^{4} ) = 4[/tex]

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

²log8 - ²log3 + ²log6 = ²log((8 x 6 )/3)= ²log16 = ²log2⁴ = 4 x ²log2 = 4 x 1 = 4
